Card Network Program

Amex Opt Blue

Amex processed directly through your merchant account, same as Visa and Mastercard. No separate Amex agreement required for merchants under $3M in annual Amex volume (updated from $1M, April 2026). One statement, one deposit schedule, no surprises.

Surcharging Eligible

Amex Opt Blue is eligible for surcharging alongside Visa, Mastercard, and Discover. All four networks, one program.

Who It Helps

Merchants currently on a direct Amex ESA agreement or those who've never accepted Amex due to complexity.

Best for: Any merchant accepting Visa/MC who wants simplified Amex acceptance without a separate agreement.

Mastercard Program

Mastercard Level 2/3

Mastercard commercial, corporate, and purchasing cards can qualify for reduced interchange when accurate transaction data is passed at the time of sale.

Eligibility: Data Rate 3 (Level III) is only available to Large Market merchants. All other B2B merchants qualify for Data Rate 2 (Level II) only.

How It Works

Gateways including CardPointe, NMI, and Valor support passing the required line-item data on qualifying Mastercard commercial card transactions. Accurate values are required to qualify for the reduced rate.

Visa's Equivalent

Visa retired Level 2 and Level 3. Visa's commercial card data program is now CEDP. See the Visa CEDP tab.

Best for: B2B merchants processing Mastercard commercial, corporate, or government purchasing cards.

New Visa Program

Visa CEDP New Apr 2026

The Commercial Enhanced Data Program replaces Visa Level 2 and Level 3. Verified merchants qualify for Product 3 rates, which can be up to 15 bps better than old L3 rates. Visa AI now validates data quality, so placeholder data no longer qualifies.

Retired
Visa Level 2 retired April 2026. Merchants relying on old L2 rates need to migrate.
What Changed
Visa AI validates transaction data quality. Dummy or placeholder values no longer earn reduced rates.
Opportunity
Verified CEDP merchants can earn up to 15 bps better than old L3. CardPointe handles qualification automatically.

Mastercard L2/L3

Mastercard's Level 2 and Level 3 programs are unchanged. CEDP is a Visa-only update.

Your Talking Point

Processors who pass placeholder data just lost those savings. Ask prospects if their current processor has confirmed CEDP compliance.

Best for: B2B merchants with Visa commercial card volume who want to confirm they are capturing CEDP rates post-April 2026.
