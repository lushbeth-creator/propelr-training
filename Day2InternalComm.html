Internal Communication: What to Expect
Status lives in Stratus first. Once boarding starts, you'll also get email notifications as the file moves, confirmation the file is complete, tracking info if equipment was ordered, and a note once the account is set up and ready to process.
Start with the Employee Portal and this page. For anything Stratus can't answer, open a ticket in the Helpdesk rather than emailing individual team members, it's what keeps your request inside the SLA.
Set the timeline at the close so nothing feels like a surprise later, when boarding will reach out, roughly how long approval takes, and when to expect the welcome call once the account is live. A merchant who knows what's coming stays calmer if something gets pended.
